About Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. In an era of rapid digital transformation, it serves as the critical layer of protection for personal privacy and national infrastructure.

CIA Triad

The core of security: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability. Ensuring data is private, accurate, and accessible when needed.

Network Security

Protecting the integrity of data and the network itself from intrusions using firewalls and encryption.

Information Security

Focusing on the protection of information assets from unauthorized use, disclosure, or destruction.
